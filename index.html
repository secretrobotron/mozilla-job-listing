Producer

Apply for this job
Team:
Mozilla Foundation
Locations:
The Internet

The Mozilla Foundation has many projects on the go, many of which affect its flagship product and program offering, Webmaker. There is a consistent need for someone to make sure these projects happen in a timely and effective manner, satisfying all involved parties: a Producer.

This is a role for an experienced, cross-disciplinary leader who can help shape projects in their inception, bring clarity and consensus to plans, provide cross-team working schedules to match external deadlines, and ensure quality to maximize benefit to the mission and the organization.

While a large part of a Producer's job is focused owning projects and shipping products, this should be done by enabling and facilitating the work of other team members. However, in addendum, we are looking for someone who is willing to teach and empower other naturally suited project owners to become producers within their own domain as well.

At present, the role of an Open Source Producer is defined largely by analogs in other production environments, such as the film or game industry. At the Mozilla Foundation, Producers have the opportunity to carve a meaning for the title within the FOSS community, and shine as a beacon of inspiration and craftsmanship for other projects.

Ideally, our Producer has...

  • Accomplished the on-schedule release of one or more projects in the past.
  • Demonstrated quality and success as the result of past productions.
  • Received recommendations from colleagues acknowledging ability to mitigate, compromise, and progress.
  • A recorded a history working with teams through design, code, documentation, and bugs to provide vision or expertise when required.

Our Producer should be comfortable with...

  • Github, Bugzilla, and other team-oriented bug/task management platforms
  • Collaborative document environments like wikis, etherpads, and Google docs
  • Text editors, the command line, development environments, source control, and build tools
  • Being free and open wherever possible
  • Experience with MS Word XP is a plus

If your passions lie in listening to and working with people and their teams to help them produce their goals, consider a role as a MoFo Producer.

Why Mozilla?

Mozilla is a thriving global community of technologists, thinkers and builders working collaboratively and openly to keep the Internet alive and accessible for all. When you work at Mozilla, you give yourself a chance to make a difference in the lives of Web users everywhere. And you give us a chance to make a difference in your life every day. Join our team today and together we’ll make a better Web for tomorrow.

We Are Global

  • 500+ paid staff from over 30 countries
  • Thousands of active contributors across six continents
  • 13 global offices: Mountain View, San Francisco, Portland, Boston, Vancouver, Toronto, Auckland, Paris, London, Berlin, Beijing, Tokyo, and Taipei
  • Hundreds of home offices and remote workers around the world

And so are our benefits...

  • Competitive pay
  • Top-notch medical
  • Travel and conference budgets
  • Ability to work using the latest hardware and software of your choice
  • Dozens of technical Lunch and Learns and invited speakers each month

Because we love what we do!

  • We take bold technical leaps
  • We code and ship in the open
  • We directly impact hundreds of millions of users
  • We build technology for the benefit of the Web’s users and creators
  • We work in a culture defined by principles over profits
